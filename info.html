ZUKO'S FAMILY
Name Relation to Zuko
Iroh Zuko's uncle, and rightful heir to the throne. He shows unconditional love and support to Prince Zuko, even after Zuko betrays him.
Azula Zuko's sister, and manipulator. She convinces Zuko to betray their uncle with false promises of unconditional love from their father.
Ozai Zuko's father, and the origin of the daddy issues. Ozai is extremely abusive to Zuko. Ozai challened 10 year old Zuko to an Agni Kai, or a Fire Duel. This is how Zuko got his scar.
Ursa Zuko's Mother, kind at heart. Ursa disappeared from Zuko's life when he was young. Little did Zuko know that she banished herself to save her son.
